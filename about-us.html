Our Story

The Inception

January 2025

It all began in our cybersecurity course, serving as the foundation for both our midterm and final projects. Our objective was to develop an e-learning website that facilitates the exploration of key cybersecurity concepts.

The Development

March 2025

We began building Cybersec Academy as a project, focusing on creating an interface that would make learning about cybersecurity both accessible and engaging.

The Progress

March 2024

After making a progress of our project, we are proud to present the early version of Cybersec Academy. With approximately 50% of the platform completed, it features initial interactive modules on basic security concepts, with more enhancements on the way.

Project Completion

Finished

We've successfully completed the Cybersec Academy platform with all planned modules. Our learning platform now includes interactive content on Password Theft, Network Security, Cloud Security, and many other critical cybersecurity topics. The learning experience is enhanced with progress tracking, quizzes, and a user-friendly interface.

Midterm Project

Cybersecurity Tips Website Part 1 – Create a website providing information about cybersecurity, including a landing page and navigation pages for Attack Targets, Phishing Attacks, and Malware Infections. The website includes an overview of each topic, how they are used, and protection methods against these attacks.

January – March 2025

Final Project

Cybersecurity Tips Website Part 2 – Complete the Cybersecurity Tips Website by adding more topics, specifically Password Thefts, Account Access Tips, Network Tapping, Wireless Network Pirating, Attacks in the Cloud, and Encryption Cracking.
